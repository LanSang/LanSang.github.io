Lan Sang

PhD Candidate, Information Systems

Leeds School of Business, University of Colorado Boulder

Welcome to my website! I’m Lan Sang, a 4th-year PhD candidate in Information Systems at the Leeds School of Business, University of Colorado Boulder. My research focuses on Psychometrics, IS Healthcare, and Social Media Analysis, where I explore the measurement of psychological constructs, the intersection of information systems and healthcare, and the role of social media in shaping public opinion and behavior. I’m fortunate to be advised by Professor Kai Larsen and Professor David Dobolyi. Before joining Leeds, I earned a Master’s degree in Computational Linguistics from CU Boulder, and I hold a Bachelor of Arts in French and a Bachelor of Business Administration from Nanjing Normal University.

Research Interests:

  • Psychometrics
  • IS Healthcare
  • Social Media Analysis

Research Methodologies:

  • Design Science
  • Large Language Models
  • Deep Learning
